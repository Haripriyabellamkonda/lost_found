Frequently Asked Questions

  • How to report a lost item?

    Reporting a lost item on our website is quick and easy:

    1. Click on the "report Lost Items" section in the navigation menu.
    2. Fill out the simple form with details about your lost item, including its description, where you last had it, and any identifying features.
    3. Submit the form, and your lost item will be added to our database.
    4. you can check "search all items" tab, if a matching found item is reported, helping you reunite with your valuable possession.

  • Can I report multiple lost items?

    Absolutely! You can report multiple lost items using the same process. Each item will be entered into our system to help maximize the chances of recovery.

  • How can I get in touch if I need assistance?

    If you have any questions or need help, please visit our "Contact Us" page or send an email to "lostandfound@gmail.com" .We're here to assist you.
